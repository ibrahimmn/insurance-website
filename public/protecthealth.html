Solutions to protect your health


You’re passionate about your business, so you’ll want to protect it in the best way. We - at protectnow Commercial Insurance - are passionate about supporting you with your risk management beyond exemplary risk transfer and claims handling to help you build resilience.

Our health affects every single area of our lives.

It's simple. Life is better when you are healthy. we all know that.
But sometimes we struggle to prioritize our health. We spend our time focusing on the future and forgetting what we need now. Taking care of yourself is often at the bottom of your to-do list. The World Health Organization (WHO) has found that lifestyle diseases (heart attack, stroke, diabetes, some cancers, and other illnesses) account for over 70% of deaths worldwide.
something has to change. Now is the time for a new approach.
Living well goes beyond body and mind. In fact, WHO defines health as a state of complete mental, social and physical well-being, not just the absence of illness or disease . Social, mental, physical and financial health are important to living a truly healthy life. 

  • Physical health

    Exercising regularly and eating mindfully.

  • Mental health

    Managing stress and self-reflecting.

  • Social health

    Nurturing relationships and engaging in our communities.
